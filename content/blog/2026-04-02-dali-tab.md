## Isolation

The DALI functionality now lives behind its own service route. The underlying MQTT subscription, state cache, REST endpoints, and Socket.IO events remain intact.

## Benefit

This keeps the operational code path clear. The DALI tab owns live controls, while the rest of the website can evolve independently.
