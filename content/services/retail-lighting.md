## Retail Lighting Intelligence Platform

**SpiderWeb Retail Lighting** gives store managers and operations teams a single dashboard to see and control every light across every store — without touching a switchboard.

---

## Why Retail Lighting Matters

Bad lighting costs money in three ways:
- **Energy waste** — lights on full brightness at 2am when the store is closed
- **Lost sales** — dim or dead zones make products look unappealing
- **Emergency repairs** — a fault that goes unnoticed for days damages brand reputation

SpiderWeb solves all three by connecting your DALI lighting network to a web dashboard your team already knows how to use.

## What Store Managers Actually Do With It

### 1. Open & Close Store Lights in One Tap

Instead of walking to a switchboard, the manager opens the app, selects the store, and taps **Open Scene** or **Close Scene**. All zones respond in seconds.

> "We used to spend 20 minutes every morning walking the store checking lights. Now it's one button." — Store Manager, VivoCity branch

### 2. Spot Faults Before Customers Complain

If a promo spotlight fails at 9am, the dashboard shows a red alert immediately. The maintenance team gets notified via Telegram before 10 customers walk in.

### 3. Cut Energy by 18% Without Changing a Single Bulb

By switching to scheduled dimming and the right scene presets, most stores can reduce lighting energy by 15–22% — no hardware changes needed.

---

## How It Works

1. Your existing DALI bus connects to a gateway (SpiderWeb Edge Node)
2. The gateway talks to SpiderWeb Cloud via MQTT
3. Your team accesses the dashboard from any browser or phone
4. Alerts go to Telegram, email, or SMS

**No app to install. No VPN needed.**
